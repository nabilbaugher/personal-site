Now / Current work

A live section for the site. Statuses below are pulled from a single data file so the page updates as the work moves — no rewriting by hand. Toggle the three layouts above. Content is real, drawn from your repos; correct anything that's off.

A — The Ledger
Closest to your home page. Quiet rows; click any to expand. Best when the list stays short and you want it to feel like the rest of the site.
B — Status cards
More surface area per project: headline metrics and the latest update visible at a glance. Best for showing momentum to a visitor who doesn't click.
C — Activity stream
Reverse-chronological feed across all projects, like a changelog. Best fit for "progress shows up without me writing about it" — each commit or log entry can append a line.
